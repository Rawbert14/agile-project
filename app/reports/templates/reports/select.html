Provided below is a tool designed primarily for team managers, aiding them in identifying issues pertaining to specific production lines or projects. At the conclusion of each day, managers can select a project and access its detailed report. This report encompasses every logged task, the time invested in each task, and an assessment of whether the overall work for the day exceeded the expected duration. This information helps pinpoint potential problems related to task descriptions, estimations, or employee-related challenges, necessitating guidance.

Notably, this analysis can be performed retrospectively for any past day, allowing for a comprehensive historical assessment. The tool generates a succinct output categorizing projects as either 'Doing ok' for smoothly progressing endeavors or 'Not Doing ok' for projects encountering developmental issues. Additionally, in the lower section, any problems logged by employees during their workday are highlighted for managerial attention.
