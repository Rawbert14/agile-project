About Agile App


The Agile Teams App is an internet-based platform designed to facilitate smoother integration for new employees joining large agile teams. It achieves this by offering various features such as a shared to-do list, question board, daily reports, project outlook, and a dedicated blog learning area.

Projects


The Projects section enables users to choose their ongoing project and track its progress, log completed tasks, report on their outcomes, create issues or challenges, and manage the tasks they've generated by editing or deleting them as needed.

Questions Board


The question board provides users with the ability to pose queries, view public issues faced by others in the workplace, and engage socially. Users can express appreciation for posts, provide comments if they aren't flagged as problems, and, if they initiated the post, they also have the option to remove it.

Get Report


The "Get Report" function enables users to choose a specific production line or project and select the corresponding workday to review the encountered challenges or successful outcomes. This feature aims to instill greater confidence in new team members during their onboarding process by offering insights into the problems tackled or confirming smooth operations on a given day.

Learning Area


The Learning Area functions as a blog-style section where users can access articles covering various topics. This resource allows new employees to explore the knowledge and expertise accumulated by seasoned team members, enabling them to expand their understanding and skills.

To Do List


This section features a collective To-Do List accessible to all team members, offering a comprehensive view of the tasks designated for the entire team rather than focusing solely on individual responsibilities. This enables new employees to understand the overall project assignments and identify opportunities to contribute and support the team's objectives.

Team Section


The team section helps the new-joinners to meet their colleagues and see how they can contact them, which team/department they are part of and also their bio where everyone should list their strong points.
